Going Forward

Our project is being designed with future growth and usability in mind. We imagine that software of this type would bring Texture Based Segmentation to a range of untapped medical applications. A surgeon preparing for a procedure could load this project as an app for his VR head set and use it to hide certain layers of tissues that are less pertinent. This would allow greater visibility of the important layers of tissues identified by their textures or visible properties.
